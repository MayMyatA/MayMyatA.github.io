Final Project: Reaction Time Game

A reaction time game tests your ability to quickly respond to stimuli. In this game, the screen will turn green and you must react as quickly as possible. The faster you react, the lower the measured time (in ms)


Link

Parts List

Arduino uno

1.77" TFT screen

Rocker switch

Mini button

150ohm resistor & 33uF capacitor

The housing is laser cut and the mounting for the screen,switch and button are 3D printed and all connections to the arduino and screen are connected using pins and all other connections are soldered
